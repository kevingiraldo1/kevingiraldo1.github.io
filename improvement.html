Workout Tips


  • Pick workouts you like
  • You can workout anywhere don't giver yourself excuses to back out, you dont need the gym or a certain barbell to just workout
  • Workouts dont have to be long
  • At least two to three days minimal for strength building
  • At least 3 days for cardio 15min-30min
  • Keep trying just because you stopped working out for a while doesnt mean you should give up, keep trying it gets easier to stick to your training
  • If you want abs you need to focus more on being lean and diet. Ab excercises will help muscles become more defined but you need lower body fat% to show them

    Diet

    Dieting should allow you to eat overall healthy and still enjoy foods you love without feeling hungry. Dieting without excercise will make things difficult , since you can easily go over calorie count and control craving since you cant enjoy foods as much. There is a multitude of information on different strategies and different diet plans. In the end picking what you like is best and keep in mind if you work out , you need to eat well for fuel.

    Tips

  • Eat something before you got out it'll help with impulses.

  • Find easy good food to make , you don't want cooking to be a chore have a simple system or your gonna find yourself buying out because it takes to long

  • Eat filling food , hunger will cause you to binge

  • Cheat Meal 1-2 times in a week or every two weeks

  • Practice mindfullness if you end up eating pretty bad think about what your eating and what does it do for you..etc.

  • Don't fall for deals you dont need a whole pizza , you can just buy a slice or two wastiing more money for a lot more is only hurting you.

  • Drink a lot more water its good for you and its filling

  • Find low calorie foods to enjoy no worries
